About GeneTrace

At GeneTrace, we believe your DNA is more than data — it’s your story, your identity, and your roots.

Founded by Sara Alajmi, Suad Alsayed, and Farah Alajmi, GeneTrace was created to protect that story in an age where cyber threats are rapidly evolving.

Our project shines a spotlight on the growing risks surrounding ancestry testing and genetic information. Through education on DNA encryption, insights into cybersecurity best practices, and real-world case studies, we aim to empower you to protect your most personal data.

GeneTrace also brings innovation to your ancestry journey. Our platform allows you to:
• Compare the security and accuracy of major ancestry testing providers
• Explore where your roots originate from across global regions
• Enjoy a fun celebrity DNA match feature, connecting you to well-known figures who may share genetic traits with you

At GeneTrace, we blend science, security, and discovery — helping you trace your origins while keeping your genetic identity safe.

Your genes tell your story. We’re here to make sure you stay the author.
