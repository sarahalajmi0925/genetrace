Security Tips

Protect your genetic identity by following these cybersecurity tips:

  • Use strong, unique passwords for your DNA accounts.
  • Enable two-factor authentication where available.
  • Be cautious when sharing your DNA information online.
  • Choose reputable genetic testing providers.
  • Encrypt your DNA data when storing it digitally.
  • Understand the terms of service and privacy policies of DNA companies.
